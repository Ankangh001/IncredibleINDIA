Thiruvananthapuram (or Trivandrum) is the capital of the southern Indian state of Kerala. It's distinguished by its British colonial architecture and many art galleries.

MUMBAI

It has the highest number of millionaires and billionaires among all cities in India. Mumbai is home to three UNESCO World Heritage Sites: the Elephanta Caves, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus, and the city's distinctive ensemble of Victorian and Art Deco buildings.

BENGALURU

Bangalore is famous being the Silicon Valley of India, Karnataka's capital city is a well known IT hub and some of the world's major It corporations operate out of the city. Apart from MNCs, Bangalore is also home to many startups and Indian tech companies.
